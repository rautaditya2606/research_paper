2. CRITICAL FAILURE ANALYSIS: INT8 CALIBRATION
----------------------------------------------
Device: Jetson Nano (Maxwell GPU)
Error Code: Error[1]: Cuda Runtime (unspecified launch failure)

Observations:
1. Success: MobileNetV3 is now successfully running in FP32/FP16 (likely following the opset adjustment to Opset 11).
2. Failure: All models failed INT8 calibration with "unspecified launch failure". On 4GB Jetson devices, this is typically a resource exhaustion (Out of Memory) or driver timeout issue during the intensive calibration pass.

Research Insight: 
The Jetson Nano's shared memory (4GB for both CPU/GPU) is a bottleneck for on-device INT8 quantization calibration. For the research paper, this highlights the necessity of "Host-Side Calibration" (calibrating on a PC and moving the INT8 Cache to the Edge) instead of "On-Device Calibration".
